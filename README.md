### coin-pusher
A React-Native coin pusher project

### UI尺寸
* 应用内单位:
  layout size (dp)
* 实际尺寸:
  pixel size (px)

* 像素密度:
* PixelRatio.get() === 1
  mdpi Android devices (160 dpi)
* PixelRatio.get() === 1.5
  hdpi Android devices (240 dpi)
* PixelRatio.get() === 2
  iPhone 4, 4S
  iPhone 5, 5c, 5s
  iPhone 6
  xhdpi Android devices (320 dpi)
* PixelRatio.get() === 3
  iPhone 6 plus
  xxhdpi Android devices (480 dpi)
* PixelRatio.get() === 3.5
  Nexus 6
